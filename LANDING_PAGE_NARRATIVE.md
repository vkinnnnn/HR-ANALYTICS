## Section 5: Feature Breakdown (Longer Version)

### Dashboard
- **KPIs:** Headcount, turnover rate, avg tenure, flight risk employees
- **Charts:** Headcount trend, turnover heatmap, tenure distribution, flight risk watchlist
- **Filters:** Department, grade, location, function, business unit
- **Real-time:** Updates daily

### Flight Risk Model
- **Features:** Tenure, time in role, manager changes, grade progression, dept turnover
- **Accuracy:** 73%
- **Output:** Risk scores (0–100%) + watchlist (top 10) + feature importance (why is this person at risk?)
- **Actionability:** 30–60 day intervention window

### Manager Analytics
- **Span of control:** Distribution across managers
- **Retention scorecard:** % of reports retained at 1yr, 2yr, 3yr
- **Advancement rate:** Promotion count per manager per year
- **Peer comparison:** Manager A vs. Manager B (identify best practices)

### Career Progression
- **Promotion velocity:** Avg days between title changes (by level, by dept)
- **Stuck talent:** People 3+ years in same role
- **Career paths:** How do people advance? What blockers exist?
- **Lateral vs. upward:** Move types (growth vs. stagnation)

### AI Chatbot
- **Interface:** Natural language Q&A
- **Examples:**
  - "What's our attrition rate in Sales?"
  - "Who's at flight risk in my department?"
  - "Show me promotion velocity trends"
  - "Which managers have the highest report retention?"
- **Output:** Text answer + relevant charts
- **Speed:** Instant (vs. 2 weeks of manual analysis)

### Reports & Export
- **Executive summary:** Auto-generated by AI (board-ready)
- **Power BI export:** Embed in existing dashboards
- **PDF reports:** Print-ready attrition analysis
- **Scheduled reports:** Weekly/monthly email digests
