## Market Definition

### The Problem: $100M+ Opportunity

**Attrition costs:**
- Average employee: $50K–$150K per departure (depending on seniority)
- Fortune 500 company (10,000 people): 15% attrition = 1,500 departures/year = $75M–$225M cost
- Tech company (2,400 people): 12% attrition = 288 departures/year = $14.4M–$43.2M cost

**Market pain points:**
1. **Invisible flight risk:** No early warning system. Lose top talent unexpectedly.
2. **Manager impact opaque:** Some teams churn 3x faster than others. Why? Unknown.
3. **Career bottlenecks:** High-potentials stuck in roles. Leave when external opportunity appears.
4. **Slow data:** Excel + HRIS admin = 2-week analysis cycle. Insights are stale.
5. **BI tool overkill:** Building custom dashboards takes 6 months + $500K. Non-technical people can't use it.
